About GrandConnect

GrandConnect is a small, family-run resource for grandparents who want practical, warm, modern advice on staying close to their grandchildren — whether they live next door or three time zones away.

Our Mission

We believe grandparents are one of the most underrated forces of stability in a child's life. Our goal is to make it easier to play, craft, gift, and connect across distance and generations — without feeling overwhelmed by technology or trends.

What We Offer

  • Easy craft and activity ideas tested with real kids
  • Age-appropriate gift guides updated each season
  • Long-distance connection rituals that actually stick
  • Calm, judgment-free advice on the emotional side of grandparenting

Who We Are

GrandConnect was started by a team of parents, grandparents, and educators in the United States. We're not a giant publisher — just people who believe that the best parenting (and grandparenting) advice is plain, kind, and usable today.
