Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

GrandConnect ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect on this website and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Contact form data: If you send us a message, we collect your name, email, and the contents of your message so we can reply.
  • Analytics data: We use Google Analytics (gtag.js) to understand which articles are useful. This includes anonymized data such as device type, browser, country, and pages visited.
  • Cookies: Google Analytics sets cookies to measure visits. We do not set our own tracking cookies.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to your messages
  • To improve our content based on what readers find helpful
  • To keep the website working and secure

Advertising

This site may display advertisements through Google AdSense and other Google ad products. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with service providers (like Google Analytics) that help us run the website.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for adult readers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Updates
