Our Services

Everything we publish is free to read. Here is what you'll find on GrandConnect:

Craft & Activity Guides

Step-by-step crafts, indoor games, and outdoor activities sorted by age and weather.

Each guide includes a materials list, time estimate, and tips for cleanup. We test every project with real grandkids before publishing.

Long-Distance Connection

Practical scripts, video-call ideas, and mailbox surprise plans for grandparents far from grandkids.

From "what do I say on a video call with a 4-year-old?" to "how do I send a care package they'll actually open?" — we cover the small details that build big bonds.

Seasonal Gift Guides

Curated picks by age, updated for birthdays, holidays, and back-to-school seasons.

We focus on gifts that last longer than a week of attention — books, building toys, experience-based gifts, and hobby starters. No fast-fad junk.

Emotional Support Articles

Guides on big feelings, family dynamics, and being a calm presence for your grandkids.

Topics include handling tantrums, navigating different parenting styles, talking about loss, and supporting kids through school stress — all written gently and without judgment.

Recipes for Cooking Together

Simple recipes designed for little hands and short attention spans.

Each recipe lists which steps are kid-safe, how to make cleanup easy, and substitution ideas for picky eaters or allergies.

Newsletter

One short, useful email every other week — new ideas, no spam.

Sign up via our contact page and we'll add you to the list. Unsubscribe anytime with one click.
